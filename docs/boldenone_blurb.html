Boldenone is a 1,4-diene derivative of testosterone. The C1-C2 double bond modification is the defining feature; it significantly decreases the rate of aromatization compared to testosterone and increases resistance to 5α-reduction. This modification results in a molecule that retains potent anabolic activity with a relatively lower androgenic profile. Because it is resistant to 5α-reductase (which normally converts testosterone to the potent DHT), it does not demonstrate the same degree of prostate or scalp androgenicity as testosterone.
