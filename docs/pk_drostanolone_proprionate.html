Absorption

After intramuscular injection, drostanolone proprionate is slowly released and hydrolysed to active drostanolone. It is highly lipophilic, extensively protein-bound, and primarily metabolised in the liver. Metabolites are excreted mainly in urine.

Distribution

Drostanolone proprionate is completely bioavailable after administration. It undergoes slow release from the depot, dissociating into drostanolone and propionic acid.

Metabolism

In patients with renal impairment, the biotransformation of drostanolone differs qualitatively from that in healthy individuals. The absolute bioavailability of drostanolone proprionate is 100%. The active ingredient, drostanolone, presumably becomes completely free from the steroid ester.

Elimination

Unchanged drostanolone proprionate is not eliminated via the kidneys. The steroid ester is completely hydrolyzed, and the resulting drostanolone is rapidly metabolized.

Excretion

Its metabolites are excreted via the kidneys (80%) and bile (20%). Drostanolone does not convert into estrogenic metabolites.
