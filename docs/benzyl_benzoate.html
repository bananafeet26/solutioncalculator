Benzyl Benzoate is a co-solvent use in parenatal formulations.

Due to it's high lipophilicity and low viscosity, it's often added to reduce the viscosity, and improve the solutions stability.

When added to carrier oils it reduces the absorption rate of the depot, effectively extending the life of the depot. [1] BB improves the stability of high concentration formulations and reduces recrystilisation at lower temperatures. [2]

For oils that experience higher viscosity at lower temperatures BB reduces the viscosity of the solution to manageable levels. [3]

Benzyl benzoate is used as a solubilising agent and nonaqueous solvent in intramuscular injections at concentrations of 0.01-46.0% v/v. [1] Long-acting intramuscular injections of ELQ-331, an antimalarial agent
[2] Crystal-free high-concentration testosterone cypionate formulations
[3] A stable pharmaceutical composition comprising testosterone undecanoate
